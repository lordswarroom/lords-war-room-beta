What is in the beta

Lords War Room is designed as a tactical control center rather than a simple helper. The beta focuses on practical tools that competitive players can test in real use.

Colosseum Tools

Review enemy lineups, explore counters, and test cleaner decision-making before you commit.

War Formations

Use structured tools for Darknest, Wonder Rally, and general war planning workflows.

Gear Planning

Track gear progression, compare setups, and use the viewer as part of your preparation flow.
